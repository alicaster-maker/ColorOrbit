Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

ColorOrbit: Sort & Build ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Anonymous Authentication: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique player ID. No personal information (name, email, phone number) is required to play.

Nickname: If you choose to set a nickname for the leaderboard, it is stored in our database linked to your anonymous ID.

Game Progress: Your current level, highest level completed, and friend connections are stored on Firebase Firestore to enable leaderboard and social features.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore) — for anonymous sign-in and game data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — for displaying rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — for in-app purchases. Transactions are handled entirely by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Leaderboard nicknames and levels are visible to other players who are your friends in the app.

4. Data Retention

Your game data is retained as long as you use the app. Since accounts are anonymous, data cannot be linked to your real identity.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal details.

6. Your Rights

You can reset your game progress at any time from within the app. If you wish to have your data deleted from our servers, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or email us at alibudak.dev@gmail.com.
