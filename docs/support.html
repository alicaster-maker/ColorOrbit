FAQ

How do I undo a move?

Tap the undo button in the top-right corner during gameplay. You start each level with 3 free undos. You can earn more by watching a rewarded ad or purchasing undo packs.

How do I restore my purchases?

Undo packs are consumable purchases and are added to your account immediately. They persist across levels and sessions.

How do I add friends?

Tap the invite button on the home screen to share your invite link. When a friend opens the link, you'll be connected automatically.

How do I reset my progress?

On the home screen, tap "New Game" to start from Level 1.
